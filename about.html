Vitor Meriat

Vitor is a computer scientist who is passionate about creating software that will positively change the world we live in.

Currently, he works as Cloud Architect and Innovator at ESX, where he is helping to shape new disruptive services based in Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence. Data science enthusiast, he works with Big Data projects, and data analytics.

Vitor is a frequent speaker at conferences in Brazil, including QCon, IoT Weekend, TDC, Azure Summit and more. He was chosen a MVP (Microsoft Most Valuable Professional) in 2016 as a recognition of her contribution to the Microsoft community.
